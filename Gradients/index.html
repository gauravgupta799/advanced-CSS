Gradient

Gradient: Display smooth transitons between two or more specific colors.

CSS defines 3 types of gradients

  • Linear Gradients ( goes down/up/left/right diagonally)
  • Radial Gradients (define by their center)
  • Conic Gradients (rotated around a center poin)

Linear Gradients

background-color: linear-gradient(direaction, color-stop1, color-stop2,.....);
